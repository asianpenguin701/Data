Lost Souls: Mapping the Haunted Connections

Overview

This map illustrates the relationship between haunted places in Kansas and nearby historic sites, cemeteries, hospitals, and population density, providing insight into potential connections among these locations.

  • The Haunted Places layer highlights locations reported to be "spoooooky."
  • The Historic Sites layer shows historically significant locations in Kansas.
  • The Haunted Places Heat Map highlights areas within Kansas with a high concentration of "spoooooky" locations.
  • The Census Demographic Map displays population densities across Kansas counties.
  • The Display Only Analysis Buffer presents an exaggerated visualization of a 1/4 mile buffer around haunted sites.
